1. Sport

Basketball

I started my journey in basketball sport since i was 15 years old when i was study at SMK Taman Setiawangsa. At that time, i met a friend named Aidil who taught and introduced to me of this sport. Since then, i had spend my most of my evening playing basketball, trying to understand and mastering the sport. It was tough, but i do believe i can achieve more in this sport. After several month, i fortunateley enlisted in my highschool basketball team to compete in major tournament at that time. It was the greatest feeling that i had feel, representing my highschool, family, and friends. To shorten the story, i had represented my highschool for over 3 years.

I continuoing my contribution and commitment in this sport after i officially registered as one of student of UiTM kampus Rembau. I met many new people with the capabality of playing basketball. I learned so much from them and i also helped several new players to mastered their skills in this sport. By the end of my fourth semester, i succesfully enlisted in UiTM Negeri Sembilan basketball team for the upcoming tournament called Karisma. Karisma can be described as the mini olympic for UiTM's institution around Malaysia.
